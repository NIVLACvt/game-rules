Here are the rules for our Minecraft server!

1. No griefing or destroying other players' builds.

2. No cheating or using hacks.

3. Be respectful to all players.

4. No cyberbullying or harassment.

Setup Instructions

1. To join on a Java Edition device:

- Open Minecraft and go to the "Multiplayer" screen.

- Click "Add Server" and enter whatever name you want for the server.

- Enter the server IP address.

- Click "Done" and then "Join Server".

2. To join on a Nintendo Switch:

- First, go to the home screen and open "System Settings".

- Go to the "Internet" tab and click "Internet Settings".

- Select your current network and click "Change Settings".

- Set "DNS Settings" to "Manual".

- Change the "Primary DNS" to 104.238.130.180 and the "Secondary DNS" to 8.8.8.8, then click "Save".

- Go back to the home screen of the Switch.

- Open Minecraft (make sure you are logged into your Microsoft account).

- Click "Play" and go to the "Servers" tab at the top of the screen.

- Join the server called "The Hive".

- After the message of the day appears (it may not always appear), select "Connect to a Server".

- In the field that says "Please enter the IP or address", enter the server IP.

- Leave the port box as it is.

- In the field labeled "(Optional) Display Name on Server List", enter the name you want to use for the server.

- Turn on "Add to server list" if you want to save it for future use.

- If you do not turn it on, you will need to repeat these steps each time.

- Click "Submit" and you will join the server.

Warning: To join on a Nintendo Switch, you will need a Microsoft account and Nintendo Online.

Have fun and enjoy playing on our server!
